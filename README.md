Lot Size Calculator 
site: https://tashet-t.github.io/positionsize_calculator/
- A professional forex and commodities trading calculator that helps determine appropriate lot sizes based on your risk management parameters.

Features
- Multiple Asset Support: Forex pairs, Gold, Silver, and Oil
- Risk Management: Calculate lot sizes based on account balance and risk percentage
- Favorite Pairs: Save frequently used pairs for quick access
- Responsive Design: Works on desktop and mobile devices
- Local Storage: Your favorite pairs are saved in your browser

How to Use
Basic Calculation
- Choose from major forex pairs (EUR/USD, GBP/USD, etc.)
- Or select commodities (Gold, Silver, Oil)
- Input your account balance in USD
- Set your risk percentage (typically 1-2% recommended)
- Enter your stop loss in pips
- Click "Calculate" to see recommended lot size
  
Results show pair, lot size, and risk amount

Example Calculation
Balance: $1,000

Risk: 1% ($10 risk)

Stop Loss: 20 pips

EUR/USD: 0.05 lots recommended

Formula
- The calculator uses standard lot size formulas:

Risk Amount = Account Balance × Risk Percentage
Lot Size = Risk Amount ÷ (Stop Loss × Pip Value)
Pip Values Used (approximated):

Major Forex Pairs: $10 per standard lot
JPY Pairs: $9 per standard lot
Gold: $1 per micro lot
Silver: $0.05 per micro lot
Oil: $10 per standard lot
